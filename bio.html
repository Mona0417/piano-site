Biography

Dr. Mengyiyi Chen earned her Doctor of Musical Arts degree in Piano Performance from the University of North Texas, where she studied with Joseph Banowetz and Steven Harlos and minored in Collaborative Piano under Elvia Puccinelli. She holds a Master of Music degree from the Manhattan School of Music and an Honorary Bachelor’s Degree from the Wuhan Conservatoire of Music.

Her debut album, Mel Bonis: Piano Solo Volume 1, released by London Toccata Classics in 2023, has garnered international acclaim for its artistic depth. Featured on BBC Radio 3, the recording has been praised for its elegant phrasing and colorful tonal nuance, establishing Dr. Chen as a thoughtful interpreter of French piano repertoire.

A winner of multiple international competitions—including the Helen Taylor Johannsen (U.S.) and Castelnuovo di Garfagnana (Italy)—her performances as both a soloist and collaborative artist are recognized for their clarity and technical mastery.

Beyond the stage, Dr. Chen is a dedicated educator serving on the boards of MTNA (Seattle Chapter) and the Eastside Music Teachers Association. Her students have consistently won top honors in competitions such as the Chopin Northwest and Pacific Northwest Piano Competitions.
